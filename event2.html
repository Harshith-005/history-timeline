1969 — Apollo 11 Moon Landing

Date: July 20, 1969

Apollo 11 landed on the Moon and astronauts Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin became the first humans to walk on the lunar surface.

Why it matters

A major achievement for science, engineering, and human exploration — and a key moment in the Space Race.
