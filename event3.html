1989 — Fall of the Berlin Wall

November 9, 1989 — symbolic end of Cold War separation in Europe.

Overview

The Berlin Wall, which had separated East and West Berlin since 1961, was opened in November 1989. This event paved the way to German reunification and marked a turning point in European geopolitics.

Key facts

  • Date: November 9, 1989
  • Significance: Beginning of the end for Soviet-style regimes in Eastern Europe.
