1989 — Fall of the Berlin Wall

Date: November 9, 1989

The Berlin Wall, which had separated East and West Berlin since 1961, was opened; this paved the way for German reunification and symbolized the end of the Cold War.

Why it matters

The event represented political change across Eastern Europe and dramatic shifts in global geopolitics.
