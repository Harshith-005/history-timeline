1903 — Wright Brothers' First Flight

Date: December 17, 1903

On December 17, 1903, Orville and Wilbur Wright achieved the first controlled, sustained flight of a powered, heavier-than-air aircraft at Kitty Hawk, North Carolina.

Why it matters

This event marks the beginning of powered aviation and opened the door to modern air travel and aeronautical engineering.
